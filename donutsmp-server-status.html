Frequently asked questions

Is DonutSMP online right now?+

The status card at the top of this page pings the DonutSMP server (donutsmp.net) live. A green 'Online' indicator means the server responded and players can connect; a red 'Offline' indicator means our last check could not reach it, which usually points to a restart, maintenance, or a temporary outage. If you see 'Offline', wait a minute and reload — the check re-runs automatically.

How many players are on DonutSMP?+

The 'Players online' figure shows the current number of players connected to DonutSMP versus the server's maximum slot count, pulled straight from the live server query. It rises and falls throughout the day with peaks around evenings and weekends. For per-category rankings of those players — richest, most kills, most playtime — open the full dashboard or the leaderboards.

How often does this status update?+

This page revalidates about once every 60 seconds, and the underlying server query is cached for a few minutes to avoid hammering the live server. In practice the online/offline state and player count you see are at most a couple of minutes old. Reload the page any time to trigger a fresh check.
